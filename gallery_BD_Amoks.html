Amoks

Comic

Amoks is a sci-fi thriller comic which follows the story of a police investigation in a near future dystopian America. Cereza, the new recruit of the police office is assigned to the team in charge of the prosecution of a serial killer only targetting politicians.

Our work was displayed at the university library of Lyon, in France for an exhibition called Ratures - Scribbles - along with the artwork of the comic artist Jarco.
The goal was to introduce university students to the different step of creation of a comic, from the script draft to the colouring. I realised an A3 multicolored silkscreen print of the cover with the help of Emile Carron for the exhibition.
